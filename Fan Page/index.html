Who is Eric Fanghanel?

In Addition to Having a Good Dog...

-Eric Fanghanel is an LA based Mexican born artist creating contingent identities through assemblages - both as collage and networks - using science fiction, virtual reality, artificial intelligences and living systems. Through moments of joy and alienation, the work presents viewers with a radical otherness, inviting them to become alien. Eric is a media artist researching post-human and non-human subjectivities arising from complex decentralized networks. He is also a developer involved in JOLOCOM a Berlin based project working to develop an app aimed at the decentralization of the internet, where users own their digital identities through linked data and blockchain technology

Eric got his Bachelors in Industrial and Product Design from Universidad Iberoamericana in Mexico City. After moving to Berlin for a couple years after college, he attended UCLA Graduate School and graduated in 2018 with a Masters in Fine Arts.
